About ELECOM

The Electoral Commission (ELECOM) is an independent body tasked with overseeing elections in the country. Our primary responsibility is to ensure that elections are fair, transparent, and conducted in compliance with the law.

Our Mission

To provide an efficient and credible electoral process that reflects the will of the people, ensuring the democratic process is safeguarded at all levels.
